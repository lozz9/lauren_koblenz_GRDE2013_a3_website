Taking to others about vaping.

Are you worried about a young person who may be vaping? A conversation with them
about it might feel hard, but it's always a good idea! Here are a few helpful tips.

Start with Information

Get key facts and statistics to support
your say. Think about in advance what
you are going to say. Thinks about
questions you want to ask them.

Approach them calmly.

Make sure you are not talking to
them in an accusing way. You may
want to start the conversation after
doing a fun activity together or
when you both are in a good mood.
Keep things casual and relaxed.

Listen to them & avoid judging or lecturing.

Give them the opportunity to explain
their actions. Talking over them will
make them trust you less. Try asking questions like: "what made
you want to try?"

Don't exaggerate.

Make sure you are honest with
them about potential harms of vaping,
without exaggerating statements. Use
statistical information and case studies
to help you.
